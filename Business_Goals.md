## Subscription Audit Rules

### Audit Criteria
- [ ] Review all active subscriptions monthly
- [ ] Flag unused subscriptions (no activity 30+ days)
- [ ] Identify duplicate services
- [ ] Check for price increases

### Audit Actions
| Condition | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| Unused 30+ days | Flag for review |
| Duplicate service | Recommend consolidation |
| Price increase | Notify immediately |
| Annual renewal approaching | Remind 14 days prior |
